## 3. Directed Scope of Work (SOW)
Primary Reason for Visit: Performance Restoration Shop Visit (PRSV) triggered by Engine Health Monitoring (EHM) telemetry showing a steady decrease in Exhaust Gas Temperature (EGT) margin.
## Module Level Directives

* 
* Module 1 (Fan Module): Perform standard visual inspection. Refurbish fan blade track liners.
* Module 3 (Intermediate Pressure Compressor): Intermediate casing inspection; clean and inspect blades for erosion.
* Module 5 (High Pressure Turbine): Full Overhaul Required. Remove and replace Stage 1 and Stage 2 HPT blades due to thermal degradation.
* Module 7 (Low Pressure Turbine): Perform borescope inspection. If clearances meet current Engine Manual (EM) limits, do not disassemble. [2] 
*

## 4. Mandatory Technical Compliance (Airworthiness & Fleet Campaigns)

| Reference Number | Category | Description / Action |
|---|---|---|
| EASA AD 2025-0182 | Mandatory | Inspect HPT disk cooling air holes for micro-cracking. |
| RR SB 72-K992 | Fleet Campaign | Upgrade the Intermediate Pressure Turbine (IPT) stage 1 nozzle guide vanes to the modified variant. |
| RR SB 72-L104 | Standard | Replacement of the High-Pressure Compressor (HPC) stage 5 rotor seals. |

## 5. Material & Parts Provisioning Policy

* 
* Parts Supply: All Life Limited Parts (LLPs) and specialized Trent XWB materials will be supplied directly from Rolls-Royce Logistics (Derby).
* Material Class: New OEM parts are required for the HPT module. Serviceable Used Material (SUM) can be used for the LPC module only if pre-approved by the Rolls-Royce Fleet Engine Manager.
* Scrap Materials: All life-expired or scrapped parts must be held for 14 days for Rolls-Royce metallurgical review before local disposal.
